BSc Financial Mathematics, BSc Economics and Mathematics, BSc Business Management and Mathematics

I am a programme coordinator for these three BSc programmes. They offer a unique opportunity to study jointly at the School of Mathematics and Leeds University Business School, giving you the best of both worlds. You will gain solid quantitative knowledge studying Maths and learn how to use it to solve problems arising in business, finance and management contexts. More details can be found on the programme catalogue (select Mathematics and click Search). If you are looking for postgraduate opportunities, check our MSc programme in Financial Mathematics.
